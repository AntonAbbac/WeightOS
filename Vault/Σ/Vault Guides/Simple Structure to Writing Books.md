## ğŸ“˜ Folder Roles Explained

### 01 - Chapters

Acts as a **scene index**, not a writing space.

- Defines narrative order
- Groups and references scenes
- Represents editorial structure, not prose

---

### 02 - Scenes

The **core narrative units**.

- Each file represents a single scene
- Scenes are written independently
- Can be rearranged, reused, or cut without breaking structure

---

### 03 - Assets

Everything that supports the story but is not part of the final prose.

#### Brainstorm

- Raw, disposable ideas
- No filtering, no judgment
- Short notes meant to be explored or discarded

#### Ideas

- Surviving concepts from Brainstorm
- More structured and intentional
- Potential candidates for scenes, arcs, or worldbuilding

#### Personas

- Internal character wiki
- Tracks personality, motivations, and evolution
- Should link back to scenes where the character appears

#### Index

- Single source of truth for the story universe
- Includes:
	- World rules
	- Historical timeline
	- Major events and milestones
